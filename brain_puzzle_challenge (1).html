How To Play

Welcome to Brain Puzzle Challenge! Test your cognitive capacity through 10 distinct, engaging stages designed to trigger lateral thinking.

  • ✓ High Score: Speed, precision, level reached, and leftover timer determine your rank. Aim for the Master tier!
